Multiscale Multiphase Model for Rain-Wind-Induced Vibration

Project information: "Mechanism and Control for a Large Amplitude Oscillating Suspension Cable of Long Span Bridge Induced by Wind in Complex Flow Region" supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China by Key Projects.

  • Title: Numerical Simulations of Rain Wind Induced Vibration
  • keywords: multiphase flow, multiscale model, high-resolution for RWIV
  • Highlights:
    • A multiscale model combining VOF with Lagrange particle methods is employed.
    • Circumferentially vibrating dynamic rivulet is observed.
    • Rainwater morphology is characterized by the wind inflow velocity.
  • Reference: Cheng Peng, Li Hui, Fuster Daniel, Chen Wenli and Zaleski Stephane. Multi-scale Simulation of Rainwater Morphology Evolution on a Cylinder Subjected to Wind. Computer & Fluids. 2015, 123:112-121
